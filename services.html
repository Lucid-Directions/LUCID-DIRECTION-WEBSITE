Software Development & AI Solutions

We build iOS, Android, and web applications with integrated AI capabilities. From mobile apps to hardware integrations, we deliver complete software solutions that work.

Machine Learning Factory

We don't just use AI, we build it. Our in-house ML factory gives us the capacity to train custom AI models for local deployment, ensuring your data stays private and your applications run fast.

  • Custom Model Training: We train AI models on your specific data and use cases, not generic off-the-shelf solutions.
  • On-Device AI: Deploy trained models directly on mobile devices or edge hardware for speed, privacy, and offline capability.
  • Computer Vision: Image recognition, object detection, and visual analysis systems like SensiSnap's AI food recognition.
  • Natural Language Processing: Voice interfaces, text analysis, and conversational AI that understands context.
  • Adaptive Learning Systems: AI that learns from user behaviour over time, becoming more accurate and personalised.

Custom Software Development

Off-the-shelf software doesn't always fit. We build bespoke software solutions designed specifically for your business processes and goals, ensuring seamless integration and optimal performance.

  • Requirement Analysis & Design: Collaborating closely with you to define requirements and design intuitive, user-centric software.
  • Full-Stack Development: Expertise across the entire technology stack, from backend databases and APIs to frontend user interfaces.
  • Scalable Architecture: Building solutions designed to grow with your business, handling increased load and evolving requirements.
  • API Development & Integration: Connecting disparate systems and enabling data flow across your technology ecosystem.
  • Maintenance & Support: Providing ongoing support to ensure your custom software remains robust and up-to-date.

iOS, Android & Web Applications

Native mobile apps that feel right at home on any platform, plus web applications that scale.

  • iOS Development: Native Swift apps with deep platform integration including HealthKit, WatchOS, and Apple ecosystem features.
  • Android Development: Native Kotlin apps optimised for the full range of Android devices and form factors.
  • Web Applications: Responsive, performant web apps built with modern frameworks and best practices.
  • Cross-Platform Solutions: When it makes sense, we build apps that work across platforms without compromising quality.
  • App Store Delivery: Full support through submission, review, and launch on Apple App Store and Google Play.

Hardware Integration

Software that connects to the physical world. We build applications that interface with sensors, wearables, and custom hardware.

  • Wearable Integration: Connect with Apple Watch, Garmin, Fitbit, WHOOP, Oura, and other fitness devices.
  • IoT Applications: Build software that communicates with sensors, controllers, and embedded systems.
  • Bluetooth & Wireless: Low-energy Bluetooth connections for real-time data from physical devices.
  • Health & Fitness Data: Integration with Apple HealthKit, Google Fit, and other health platforms.
  • Custom Hardware Protocols: When off-the-shelf won't work, we develop custom communication protocols.

Software-as-a-Service (SaaS) Development

Launch your own subscription-based software product. We guide you through the entire process, from concept validation to deployment and scaling.

  • MVP Development: Building Minimum Viable Products to test your market and gather user feedback quickly.
  • Multi-Tenant Architecture: Designing platforms capable of securely serving multiple customers.
  • Subscription & Billing Integration: Implementing secure payment gateways and subscription management.
  • Cloud Deployment & Scaling: Leveraging cloud platforms (AWS, Azure, GCP) for reliable and scalable hosting.
